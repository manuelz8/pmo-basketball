Power Moves Only (PMO) Basketball Group was established to help male and female basketball players begin, plan, and grow their professional careers.

We offer professional basketball services such as career consultation, player placement, resume building, marketing, and highlights. Our clients are based all over the world and so are our connections.

Message us today to see how we can help you and your basketball career!
