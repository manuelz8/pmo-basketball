Power Moves Only

Power Moves Only (PMO) Basketball LTD was established to help grow and promote basketball in the UK as well as have a positive impact on players around the world. Our company consists of PMO Basketball Group which is geared towards professional players, and PMO Basketball Academy which is designed for amateur players.
